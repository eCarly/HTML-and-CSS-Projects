Shetland Sheepdogs known commonly as Shelties

Shelties originated from the Shetland Islands and were used by locals to herd sheep.

Shelties are commonly mistaken for Collies, but they are entirely different breeds

Color Types:

Sable, tri-color, bi-color, and merle.

Types of Work:

Herding, agility, show, therapy dogs, companions.

Once you have had a wonderful dog, a life without one, is a life diminished.
